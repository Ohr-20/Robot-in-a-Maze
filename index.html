How A* works

A* always finds the shortest path.
At every step it picks the node with the lowest score: f(n) = g(n) + h(n) g(n) — exact steps taken so far
h(n) — Manhattan distance to goal

This guarantees optimality while exploring far fewer nodes than BFS.
